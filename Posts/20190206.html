11 Resources to Develop Beautiful, Modern Websites

by Amber Beebe

Posted on February 6th, 2019 at 6:00 PM

What I am about to share with you is good. No, it’s really really good. I’ve kept these tools under lock and key, near to my heart, and almost coveted, like the tomb of the lost ark.



Maybe a bit much?

Anyways! The resources I want to share you come from years of trial and error during my short - but whirlwind time as a web developer. They are tools that every beginning, eager, misty-eyed, programmer *needs* to keep in their arsenal to succeed and ultimately create epic web content! My hope is that by sharing these tips with you, I can save you many a long night of frantic, coffee-induced stack overflow rampages, scouring the internet for, “how to center a div horizontally and vertically on the page”.

In the end, I hope you bookmark these links, read one of the books, and come away feeling less overwhelmed by all of the information available out there on “learning to code”.
